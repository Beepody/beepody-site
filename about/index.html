About Beepody

Beepody is an online community founded in 2006 which is dedicated to psychedelic research, therapy, harm-reduction, and advocacy.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to deliver peace of mind through exceptional care and avant-garde advocacy of psychotropic therapies.

Non-profit

Beepody rejects our corporatocracy and the concept of profit. Any surplus is invested in our mission to turn on the World.

Governance

Beepody is managed by an elected Board of Directors, chosen by and from our membership. The Board meets monthly, and publishes agendas of upcoming meetings and minutes of past meetings publicly.
